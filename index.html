Our History

Nyakiambi Girls Secondary School started in 1968 as a mixed secondary school. It became a girls-only school in 1988, with two streams until 2002 when the third stream was added. The fourth stream was introduced in 2009, and the fifth in 2018. It is an Extra-County School with over 800 students.

Our Location

Mirangine Sub-County, Nyandarua County, Kenya.
